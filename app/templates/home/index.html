About Shizen

Shizen is an amazing bot that can help you know more about animals, and plants. It can help connect you with animals more easily and meaningfully than ever before.

You can simply chat with it and tell about what you love, what you do and what interests you then Shizen will find an animal that matches everything you told! It will also tell you more about that animal, how is it endangered by our activities, a crazy snap and how you can help protect it!
